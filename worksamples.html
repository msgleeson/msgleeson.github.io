Below are two examples of Major Projects produced by students studying the Industrial Technology - Multimedia course.

Both students achieved a Band 6 for this course in the HSC.

Obsessed

"OBSESSED" is a music video to a self produced piece of music that addresses a loss of viewership and engagement with videos within the music industry. This student's Major Project was awarded 60/60 (full marks).

Damnation

"DAMNATION" is a thought-provoking Sci-Fi short film that delves into the final moments of an astronaut as he plummets towards an unknown planet. These fleeting seconds become a profound vessel, allowing him to reflect on and appreciate the entirety of his life experiences. This project is a synthesis of philosophical musings and intense visual storytelling, aiming to immerse the viewer in the astronaut's journey of introspection.
